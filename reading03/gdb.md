TLDR - gdb
==========

Overview
--------

[gdb] - debugging utility during execution

	$ gdb executable-name

Examples
--------

**-q** - supresses introductory and copyright
	$ gdb -q executable-name

**-h** - Lists options and gives explanations for them

	$ gdb -h executable-name
