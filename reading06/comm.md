TLDR - comm
==========

Overview
--------

[comm] - compared sorted files line-by-line

	$ comm [option] [files]

Examples
--------

**-12** - suppresses unique likes from file1 or fil2

	$ comm -12 myFile myFile2

**-3** - suppress lines files have in common

	$ comm -3 myFile myFile2
