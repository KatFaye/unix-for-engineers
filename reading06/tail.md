TLDR - tail
==========

Overview
--------

[tail] - shows last part of file

	$ tail [option] [file]

Examples
--------

**-n k** - prints last k lines instead of 10

	$ head -n 5 superFile

**-c k** - prints last k bytes instead of 10

	$ head -c 8 superFile
