TLDR - unzip
==========

Overview
--------

[unzip] - extract, test, or list compressed files in zip archive

	$ unzip archive.zip

Examples
--------

**-l** - list contents of archive
	$ zip -l archive.zip

**-n** - never overwrite existing files

	$ unzip -n superImportantArchive.zip
